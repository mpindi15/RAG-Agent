# OmniQAV Robotics — Employee Handbook (Sample)

This is a sample internal document used to demonstrate and evaluate the RAG
agent. Upload it (or any other PDF/DOCX/TXT/MD file) via the Documents tab,
then ask questions about it in the Chat tab.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

Full-time employees accrue 18 days of PTO per calendar year, credited at the
start of each quarter (4.5 days per quarter). PTO does not expire at year end;
employees may carry over up to 10 unused days into the following year. PTO
requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 5 business days in
advance for requests longer than 3 days.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week without prior approval.
Fully remote arrangements (5 days per week) require manager sign-off and are
reviewed every 6 months. All remote employees must be reachable during core
hours, 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in their local time zone, and must attend the
quarterly in-person all-hands when feasible.

## Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses under $75 can be submitted with a receipt photo through the
Expensify app and are typically reimbursed within 5 business days. Expenses
over $75 require prior manager approval. Travel expenses (flights, hotels)
must be booked through the corporate travel portal; personal bookings are
reimbursed only up to the equivalent corporate-portal rate.

## Security Requirements

All laptops must have full-disk encryption and endpoint protection enabled
before connecting to any internal system. Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
is mandatory for all company accounts, including email, VPN, and the source
code repository. Employees must complete the annual security awareness
training by December 1st; failure to do so results in automatic suspension of
VPN access until the training is completed.

## On-Call Rotation

Engineers on the Platform and Infrastructure teams participate in a weekly
on-call rotation, Monday 9:00 AM to the following Monday 9:00 AM. On-call
engineers receive a flat stipend of $400 per week and are expected to
acknowledge a page within 15 minutes. Two consecutive on-call weeks require
director approval and come with an additional day of PTO.
